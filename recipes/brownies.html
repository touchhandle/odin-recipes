Brownies Recipe

Brownies. A classic.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Stir dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  3. Pour in wet ingredients and mix until well blended.
  4. Spread mixture in a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  5. Bake 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool for about 10 minutes before serving.
