Hummus Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and washed
  • 4 tablespoons tahini
  • 4 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a food processor and blend for 5 minutes.
  2. Serve and garnish with olive oil and paprika.
